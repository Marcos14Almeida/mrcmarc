Electronics Projects

TCC Liceu Development Start: 09/2014. End: 11/2014. Project description This work aims at the implementation of electronic concepts for the creation of a project that assists the parking of an automotive vehicle. The circuit used will detect obstacles through the use of an ultrasonic sensor, which will emit sound waves that will be reflected by the object. Their return time will determine the distance between the vehicle and the obstacle. If the distance is short, a sound alert will warn the driver, thus avoiding possible accidents and damage to the vehicle.
