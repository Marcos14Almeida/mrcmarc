Unsupervised Machine Learning for Anomaly Localization in Optical Amplifiers using Real Network Telemetry Data
2024 European Conference on Optical Communication (ECOC) 28 April 2024
Marcos P. A. Dal Maso Kayol S. Mayer Andres F. Escallón-Portilla Darli A. A. Mello Dalton S. Arantes Christian E. Rothenberg

We propose an unsupervised ML approach to localize optical amplifier anomalies using input-output power time series. Tested on real optical network telemetry data, our method successfully identified a faulty amplifier.

Machine Learning time-series Optical Communications anomaly-detection

Network-Wide QoT Estimation Using SGD with Gradient Transfer Between Wavelengths.
2023 European Conference on Optical Communication (ECOC) 5 May 2023
Kayol S. Mayer Rossano P. Pinto Marcos P. A. Dal Maso Humberto V. Q. Melo Christian E. Rothenberg Darli A. A. Mello

We propose an SGD-based QoT estimation technique that operates on a network-wide scale by transferring gradients among neighboring wavelengths. Simulation results indicate effective and low-complexity QoT estimation using only transponder SNR telemetry.
